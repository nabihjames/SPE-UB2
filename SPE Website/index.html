About us

We Are SPE University of Batna 2

Established on October 15th, 2019, the SPE-University of Batna 2 Student Chapter proudly stands as the third chapter in Algeria, following OUERGLA and BOUMERDES. Since our inception, we have been dedicated to fostering a community of passionate individuals committed to the exploration, development, and production of oil and gas resources. Our chapter stands out among others due to its inclusivity, embracing a wide array of majors including Geology, Information Technology (IT), Health, Safety, and Environment (HSE), and Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM). This diversity creates a rich tapestry of expertise, fostering an environment where members can engage in interdisciplinary knowledge exchange and collaborative efforts. By incorporating such diverse fields, we provide a holistic platform that not only encourages learning within specific disciplines but also promotes cross-disciplinary collaboration, enriching the experiences and perspectives of our members.
